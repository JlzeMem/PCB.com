About This App

This application is designed to help you manage and search for information related to E2S PCB (Printed Circuit Board) details, including associated pictures and descriptions.

Product Categories:

  • Spectra: Audible & Visual
  • AlertAlarm: Audible
  • AlertAlight: Audible & Visual
  • Sonora: Audible
  • GPH: Buzzers
  • Appello: User recordable
  • M: Heavy industrial
  • SpectrAlarm: Audible & Visual
  • Hootronic: Sirens, Bells & Buzzers

Hazardous Area Classifications:

  • IS: CI D1, Zone 0
  • BEx: Zone 1, 21
  • GNEx: Zone 1, 21
  • STEx: Zone 1, 21
  • D1x: CI/II D1, Zone 1
  • D2x: CI/II D2, Zone 2
  • E2x: CI/II D2, Zone 2

Version: 1.0
Developed for E2S Warning Signals.
